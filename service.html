CYBER

Leidos defends the most targeted networks in the world from the most sophisticated attacks, and we help our customers integrate unparalleled cyber solutions tailored to protect their agency or organization's most critical information and assets. Our team of cybersecurity experts — including cleared developers, solution architects, and key management personnel — bring an established track record for delivering agile, game-changing cyber solutions to modernize the operation and maintenance of network infrastructure, virtualization and cloud migration, and enterprise applications and systems.

OPERATIONS AND LOGISTICS

C4ISR

Leidos delivers equipment, supplies, facilities, transportation, tools, materials and other items and services necessary to perform Command, Control, Communications, Computers, Intelligence, Surveillance and Reconnaissance (C4ISR) systems integration. Our mission-critical ISR systems provide support for the development, operation, and maintenance of manned, unmanned, and autonomous ISR vehicles
