About us

Leidos is a Fortune 500® information technology, engineering, and science solutions and services leader working to solve the world’s toughest challenges in the defense, intelligence, homeland security, civil, and health markets. The company’s 31,000 employees support vital missions for government and commercial customers. Headquartered in Reston, Virginia, Leidos reported annual revenues of approximately $10.17 billion for the fiscal year ended December 29, 2017.

Mission and Vision

Everything we do is built on a commitment to do the right thing for our customers, our people, and our community. Our Mission, Vision, and Values guide the way we do business.

Mission

Leidos makes the world safer, healthier, and more efficient through technology, engineering, and science.

Vision

Become the global leader in the development and application of technology to solve our customers’ most demanding challenges.

Engage, develop, and empower our diverse and valued people to foster a culture of creativity and growth.

Strengthen our communities through volunteerism, sustainable operations, and the advancement of equality.

Our values

INTEGRITY

Integrity embodies the courage to navigate challenging ethical dilemmas, the commitment to excellence in our endeavors, fostering transparency within our team, and demonstrating unwavering respect towards all individuals.

INNOVATION

Innovation transcends the realm of engineers and scientists; it serves as a catalyst for progress. It entails fostering tenacity and curiosity, propelling us toward excellence and fostering a culture of continuous learning within our organization.

COLLABORATION

Collaboration thrives on a team-oriented approach, actively pursuing collective goals. It revolves around nurturing relationships and maintaining continual connection with one another.

COMMITMENT

Commitment entails holding ourselves accountable, assuming ownership of our actions, exemplifying servant leadership, and operating with a dedicated sense of urgency in serving both our customers and teams.

Our values are embodied

by our employees

Our mission, vision, and values are demonstrated by our 47,000 employees each and every day they are helping our customers execute on important missions on the front lines of the world's most complex markets.
