Our values

INTEGRITY

Integrity embodies the courage to navigate challenging ethical dilemmas, the commitment to excellence in our endeavors, fostering transparency within our team, and demonstrating unwavering respect towards all individuals.

INNOVATION

Innovation transcends the realm of engineers and scientists; it serves as a catalyst for progress. It entails fostering tenacity and curiosity, propelling us toward excellence and fostering a culture of continuous learning within our organization.

COLLABORATION

Collaboration thrives on a team-oriented approach, actively pursuing collective goals. It revolves around nurturing relationships and maintaining continual connection with one another.

COMMITMENT

Commitment entails holding ourselves accountable, assuming ownership of our actions, exemplifying servant leadership, and operating with a dedicated sense of urgency in serving both our customers and teams.

Defense and Intelligence Core Capabilities

CYBER

Leidos defends the most targeted networks in the world from the most sophisticated attacks, and we help our customers integrate unparalleled cyber solutions tailored to protect their agency or organization's most critical information and assets. Our team of cybersecurity experts — including cleared developers, solution architects, and key management personnel — bring an established track record for delivering agile, game-changing cyber solutions to modernize the operation and maintenance of network infrastructure, virtualization and cloud migration, and enterprise applications and systems.

OPERATIONS AND LOGISTICS

Specialized platform and range support providing full spectrum aviation, ground, and maritime maintenance and operation solutions. We provide costeffective tools combined with experienced thought leadership and enhanced processes to meet global, mission-critical customer needs through product support, transportation and distribution, and logistics enablers.

C4ISR

Leidos delivers equipment, supplies, facilities, transportation, tools, materials and other items and services necessary to perform Command, Control, Communications, Computers, Intelligence, Surveillance and Reconnaissance (C4ISR) systems integration. Our mission-critical ISR systems provide support for the development, operation, and maintenance of manned, unmanned, and autonomous ISR vehicles

INTELLIGENCE ANALYSIS AND OPERATIONS

Providing the delivery of innovative solutions, essential services, and enriched analytical tools, including GEOINT, SIGINT, and HUMINT processing and exploitation, both in and out of the contiguous states, to support U.S. and coalition forces in Counter-Improvised Explosive Device (IED) operations and countering irregular warfare threats during our customers’ critical missions.
